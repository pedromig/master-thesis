\chapter{Background}
\label{ch:background}

\chquote{\textquote{If I have seen further than others, it is by standing upon the shoulders of giants.}}{Isaac Newton}{}

This chapter presents a comprehensive literature review of optimization,
meta-heuristics and modelling. Additionally, it provides a background review of
the state-of-the-art regarding the principled modelling approach. In
particular,~\Cref{sec:optimization-concepts} describes fundamental
\acrshort{combinatorial-optimization} concepts deemed relevant for better
understanding this work.~\Cref{sec:optimization-strategies} discusses multiple well-known techniques for
solving~\acrshort{combinatorial-optimization}
problems.~\Cref{sec:meta-heuristics} describes~\acrshort{meta-heuristic} methods
and presents an extensive review of~\acrshort{meta-heuristic} solvers.
Finally,~\Cref{sec:modelling} delves into the details of the modelling approach
and describes the existing implementations.
